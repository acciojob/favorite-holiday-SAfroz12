Bali is a beautiful tropical island known for its stunning beaches, lush forests, rich culture, and peaceful temples. It is one of the most popular holiday destinations for relaxation, adventure, and nature.

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Uluwatu Temple:a cliff-top temple with a breathtaking sunset view.
  2. Ubud Monkey Forest:a sacred forest filled with playful monkeys.
  3. Tanah Lot:a temple located on a rock formation surrounded by the sea.
  4. Tegallalang Rice Terraces :iconic green rice fields with beautiful patterns.
  5. Kuta Beach :famous for surfing, nightlife, and golden sand.
